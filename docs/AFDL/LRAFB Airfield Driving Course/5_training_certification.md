# Lesson 5: Training and Certification

Attachment 7 from DAFI 13-213 serves as the performance checklist for trainers, ensuring key airfield driving standards are thoroughly assessed and met during training. It guides trainers in evaluating proficiency in communication, situational awareness, and adherence to safety protocols, providing a structured approach to certify drivers are fully prepared for airfield operations.Note: Trainee and trainer digital signatures verify completion of the form. Unit ADPMs are not required to maintain a separate copy of this form, as it will remain accessible on this platform.
