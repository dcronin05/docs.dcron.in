## Definitions

* Controlled Movement Area (CMA): "Any portion of the airfield     requiring aircraft, vehicles and pedestrians to obtain specific ATC approval for access." 
* Controlled Movement Area Violation (CMAV):  "An airfield infraction caused by aircraft, vehicles, or pedestrians entering the controlled movement area without specific ATC approval."  Source: Transport CanadaThis also includes runway incursions, the most serious form of CMAVs.

!!! quote ""
    Reference: AFI 13-213, Airfield Management

## Communications

* Never assume: 
    * ATC knows who you are where, you are, or what you are doing
    * You have approval to enter the runway unless you receive a clear radio transmission from ATC
    * You know more than ATC about what aircraft are landing, taking off, or flying in the local area
    * The runway is closed unless verified through ATC or Airfield Management

!!! danger "Always get ATC approval prior to entering the runway environment!"

* **Maintain the "Big Picture":**  Monitor the radio when operating on runway and taxiways
* **Transmit Clearly:**  Plan your transmission and pay attention to standard phraseology
* **Listen Carefully:** Listen to ATC and other transmissions to increase situational awareness, don't let communications become automatic
* **Make Sure You are Heard:**  Test your radio before entering the airfield and be aware of transmission blind spots on the airfield
* **Proper Equipment:**  Due to handheld radio's limited range and battery failures install mobile radios in vehicles that frequently access runways

!!! danger "Effective communication is the key to runway incursion prevention"

Radio Terminology/Phraseology:
    * Identify yourself with your call sign
    * State location & intentions to ATC
    * Wait for ATC response
    * If you do not understand ATC response, ask them to repeat by saying "SAY AGAIN"

!!! danger "Never move your vehicle unless you are positive ATC knows what you want and has given you approval to proceed"

* Radio Terminology/Phraseology:
    * The only ATC phraseology authorizing you to enter or cross any controlled area is "PROCEED" or "CROSS"
    * Acknowledge receipt of ATC instructions by repeating instructions verbatim 
    * Notify ATC after exiting a CMA until they acknowledge the transmission

!!! danger "Always follow ATC instructions and readback verbatim"

## Ways to Reduce Incursions

* Ensure the Airfield Driving program meets AFIs/guidance
* Enforce compliance with local airfield driver instructions      
* Minimize the number of drivers authorized on the airfield
* Increase Airfield Driver's Certificate spot checks
* Identify and fix radio communication problems with vehicles
* Minimize runway crossings during increased aircraft operations
* Improve training on runway crossing procedures/phraseology
* Improve airfield signage and markings
* Develop local area information pamphlets on "high threat" areas
* More training emphasis on controlled movement area access procedures

!!! danger "We need your help to reduce runway incursions"

## What You Should Know Now

* Know where you are at all times
* Never move your vehicle unless ATC has given you approval to enter the controlled movement area
* Make sure ATC and others see you at all times
* Always follow ATC instructions and readback verbatim
* Aircraft emergencies, exercises, or incident responses require ATC approval to enter the runway

!!! danger "Stay alert, follow procedures, and always drive safely"
