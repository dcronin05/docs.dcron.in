# non_CMA Test

This test is designed to assess your knowledge and proficiency in non-CMA operations, specifically focusing on driving near runway environments. If you have not been trained or certified to operate vehicles on or near the runway, this is the appropriate test for you. Please ensure you have completed the necessary training before attempting. Once you complete the test, you will not be automatically licensed. Members must navigate back to the licensing menu and press 'Submit Application'. Only general knowledge questions can be missed; all others are auto-fail questions per DAFI 14-213.
