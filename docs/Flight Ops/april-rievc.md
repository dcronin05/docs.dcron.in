# Installation Entry/Exit Vehicle Checks (IEVC)

In accordance with AFI 31-101, paragraph 3.6.9 - 3.6.9.4, I direct the Security Forces to conduct vehicle entry/exit point checks on the below days and at the times and places specified for a period of one-half hour for the month of April 2025. If other requirements take precedence, disregard checks for that day. The intent of the checks is to protect the security of the command and to protect personnel and government property to include classified and controlled unclassified information.
